Palindrome:
==========
A palindrome is a number or letter that remains the same even if the number and letters are inverted.

Eg1:
121, 11, 414, 1221, 74747 are the palindrome numbers.
MOM, DAD, MADAM, REFER are the palindrome letters.
Hello, Hi, How are not the palindrome letters

Palindrome algorithm:
---------------------
1.Read the number or letter.
2.Hold the letter or number in a temporary variable.
3.Reverse the letter or number.
4.Compare the temporary variable with reverses letter or number.
5.If both letters or numbers are the same, print "this string/number is a palindrome."
6.Else print, "this string/number is not a palindrome."
